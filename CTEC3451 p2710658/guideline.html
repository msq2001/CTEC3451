Buyer Guidelines

1. Introduction

Welcome to our marketplace! As a buyer, it's important to familiarize yourself with our guidelines to ensure a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience. By following these guidelines, you can confidently make purchases, knowing that you are protected and supported throughout the entire process.

2. Account Safety

To ensure the safety of your account, make sure to use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication if available. Never share your password or account details with anyone, and always log out after completing your shopping session, especially on public devices.

3. Payment and Shipping

When making a purchase, always use the available payment methods on our platform for maximum security. Familiarize yourself with the shipping options provided by the seller and choose the one that best suits your needs. Keep in mind that shipping times may vary depending on the seller's location and the shipping method chosen.

4. Returns and Refunds

Before making a purchase, review the seller's return and refund policy. In case you are not satisfied with the product received, contact the seller to discuss the issue and arrange a return or refund if applicable. Always follow the platform's guidelines when requesting a return or refund to ensure a successful resolution.

5. Dispute Resolution

If you encounter any issues with your purchase that cannot be resolved directly with the seller, you may file a dispute through our platform. We will review the details of the case and work with both parties to reach a fair resolution. Remember to provide all relevant information and documentation to support your claim.
